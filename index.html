What is online safety?

Online safety also known as internet safety or cybersecurity,refers to the practices and precaution taken to protect one self and others from risks and threats that exist in the online enviroment.This includes safe guarding personal information, avoiding harmful content,and protecting against cyber threats such as hacking,phising and identity theft.

Online safety involves=

  1. Privacy protection
  2. Secure browsing
  3. Safe communication
  4. Digital footprint management
